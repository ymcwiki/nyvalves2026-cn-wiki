# Coronary Artery Obstruction

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/393-successful-pci-and-tavr-for-high-coronary-artery|393-successful-pci-and-tavr-for-high-coronary-artery]]

## 定义
TAVR 过程中瓣叶或瓣架遮挡冠脉开口导致的冠脉阻塞风险，本病例以左冠高度低（9.4 mm）、瓣叶长（11.8 mm）和 VTC 距离小（3.0 mm）作为高风险指标。
