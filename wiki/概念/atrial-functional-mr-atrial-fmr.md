# atrial functional MR (atrial FMR)

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/273-optimizing-procedural-m-teer-outcomes-balancing|273-optimizing-procedural-m-teer-outcomes-balancing]]、[[来源/097-case-resolution|097-case-resolution]]

## 定义
由心房扩张和瓣环扩张引起的功能性二尖瓣反流亚型，MPG 的预后信号在此亚型中显著，而在 ventricular FMR 中不明显。
