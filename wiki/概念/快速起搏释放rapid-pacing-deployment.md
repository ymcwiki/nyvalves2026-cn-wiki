# 快速起搏释放（rapid pacing deployment）

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/861-case-resolution|861-case-resolution]]、[[来源/862-case-presentation|862-case-presentation]]、[[来源/555-modified-unicorn-assisted-tavr-for-severe-aortic|555-modified-unicorn-assisted-tavr-for-severe-aortic]]、[[来源/048-case-resolution|048-case-resolution]]、[[来源/170-rapid-ventricular-pacing-to-optimize-ttvr-device|170-rapid-ventricular-pacing-to-optimize-ttvr-device]]、[[来源/304-triple-transcatheter-aortic-valve-replacement-va|304-triple-transcatheter-aortic-valve-replacement-va]]

## 定义
TAVR手术中通过临时心脏起搏器快速起搏降低心输出量，以稳定瓣膜定位并减少位移，本例Jena Trilogy THV即采用此技术释放。
