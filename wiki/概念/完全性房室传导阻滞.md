# 完全性房室传导阻滞

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/477-when-the-clip-drops-the-beat-complete-heart-bloc|477-when-the-clip-drops-the-beat-complete-heart-bloc]]、[[来源/833-torrential-mitral-regurgitation-following-self-e|833-torrential-mitral-regurgitation-following-self-e]]、[[来源/757-permanent-pacemaker-recovery-post-transcatheter|757-permanent-pacemaker-recovery-post-transcatheter]]、[[来源/271-edge-of-embolization-snare-mediated-sapien-3-sep|271-edge-of-embolization-snare-mediated-sapien-3-sep]]

## 定义
心脏传导系统中 AV 结功能完全中断，导致心室无法接收心房电信号，本病例发生于三尖瓣前隔交界区 TEER 操作后。
