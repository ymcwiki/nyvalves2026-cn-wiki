# HAT分级

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/027-ct-assessment-of-device-related-thrombosis-is-th|027-ct-assessment-of-device-related-thrombosis-is-th]]、[[来源/090-what-do-i-do-for-massive-device-related-thrombus|090-what-do-i-do-for-massive-device-related-thrombus]]、[[来源/196-imaging-post-procedural-complications-the-basics|196-imaging-post-procedural-complications-the-basics]]

## 定义
HAT分级（Hypoattenuating/Hypoattenuated Thickening分级）是Korsholm等于2024年提出的CT评估系统，用于对左心耳封堵（LAAO）术后器械表面的低衰减增厚结构进行标准化分类。系统按0至3级划分：Grade 0无异常增厚，Grade 1为低危表现，Grade 2-3为高危HAT/DRT，分级依据包括增厚厚度、与左房壁的连续性及形态不规则程度。该分级旨在区分正常愈合组织与真正血栓，为术后抗凝决策提供影像学依据。
