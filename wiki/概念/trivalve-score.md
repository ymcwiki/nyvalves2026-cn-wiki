# TRIVALVE score

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/526-what-is-the-appropriate-trial-design-for-patient|526-what-is-the-appropriate-trial-design-for-patient]]、[[来源/725-clinical-significance-of-the-tri-score-following|725-clinical-significance-of-the-tri-score-following]]、[[来源/854-challenges-of-clinical-assessment|854-challenges-of-clinical-assessment]]、[[来源/135-t-varc-2-guidelines-new-tricuspid-therapies|135-t-varc-2-guidelines-new-tricuspid-therapies]]

## 定义
TRIVALVE score 是专为经导管三尖瓣介入（TTVI）设计的术前风险评分工具，基于5个临床变量，分值≥2.5对应高风险，已被T-VARC-2框架纳入作为无效性阈值参考。该评分用于预测1年死亡或再住院复合终点，但多项研究显示其在当代TTVI人群中区分度有限，AUC介于0.61至0.658之间，低于TRI-SCORE（AUC 0.692），且倾向于低估实际事件率。
