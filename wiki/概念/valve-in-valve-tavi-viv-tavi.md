# valve-in-valve TAVI (ViV-TAVI)

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/274-lessons-from-surgical-bioprosthetic-valve-failur|274-lessons-from-surgical-bioprosthetic-valve-failur]]、[[来源/324-decision-making-in-lifetime-management-of-aortic|324-decision-making-in-lifetime-management-of-aortic]]

## 定义
在退化的外科生物瓣内经导管植入新瓣膜的技术，Trifecta 再干预队列中 796/1373 例接受 ViV-TAVI，早期生存优于 repeat SAVR。
