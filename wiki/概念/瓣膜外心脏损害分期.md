# 瓣膜外心脏损害分期

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/421-staging-classification-of-primary-mitral-regurgi|421-staging-classification-of-primary-mitral-regurgi]]、[[来源/535-invasive-pressure-volume-loop-assessment-of-acut|535-invasive-pressure-volume-loop-assessment-of-acut]]

## 定义
基于 LV/LA 损害、肺高压/TR、RV 衰竭四级分期体系，用于评估 MR 患者心外结构损害程度并预测预后，Stage 1-4 逐步加重。
