# 经颈动脉 TAVR

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/108-transcarotid-valve-in-valve-tavr-and-percutaneou|108-transcarotid-valve-in-valve-tavr-and-percutaneou]]、[[来源/537-challenging-thv-sizing-in-bicuspid-aortic-valve|537-challenging-thv-sizing-in-bicuspid-aortic-valve]]

## 定义
通过颈动脉入路完成经导管主动脉瓣置换的替代途径，本病例因严重双侧 PAD 致股动脉入路不可行而采用右颈总动脉 cutdown（14F 鞘）完成 ViV TAVR。
