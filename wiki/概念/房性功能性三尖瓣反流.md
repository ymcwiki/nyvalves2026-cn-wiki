# 房性功能性三尖瓣反流

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/170-rapid-ventricular-pacing-to-optimize-ttvr-device|170-rapid-ventricular-pacing-to-optimize-ttvr-device]]、[[来源/834-case-presentation-1-moderate-severe-tr-with-symp|834-case-presentation-1-moderate-severe-tr-with-symp]]、[[来源/344-case-discussion-2-atrial-secondary-tr|344-case-discussion-2-atrial-secondary-tr]]

## 定义
由心房扩张（尤其是长期房颤导致的右心房扩大）引起三尖瓣瓣环扩大而产生的功能性三尖瓣反流，本例在节律控制后完全逆转。
