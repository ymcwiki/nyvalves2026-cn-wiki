# 溶血性心衰

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/187-when-anchoring-fails-valve-in-valve-rescue-for-s|187-when-anchoring-fails-valve-in-valve-rescue-for-s]]

## 定义
重度PVL通过机械性红细胞破坏引起溶血，进而加重心衰，本例中表现为血红蛋白最低6.5 g/dL和haptoglobin降低。
