# 医源性房间隔缺损（ASD）封堵

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/832-hostile-anatomy-without-options-challenging-case|832-hostile-anatomy-without-options-challenging-case]]、[[来源/102-how-to-navigate-across-an-asd-occluder-to-perfor|102-how-to-navigate-across-an-asd-occluder-to-perfor]]

## 定义
PBMC 后经房间隔穿刺点残余开放导致右向左分流时的紧急处理措施，本例因重度肺高压使分流严重，需紧急封堵。
