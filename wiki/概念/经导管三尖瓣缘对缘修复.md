# 经导管三尖瓣缘对缘修复

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/092-t-teer-update-on-the-clinical-evidence|092-t-teer-update-on-the-clinical-evidence]]、[[来源/810-case-presentation-1-favorable-tr-anatomy-repair|810-case-presentation-1-favorable-tr-anatomy-repair]]、[[来源/842-do-we-need-more-objective-risk-scores|842-do-we-need-more-objective-risk-scores]]、[[来源/074-transjugular-transcatheter-edge-to-edge-tricuspi|074-transjugular-transcatheter-edge-to-edge-tricuspi]]、[[来源/471-transcatheter-tricuspid-edge-to-edge-repair-in-r|471-transcatheter-tricuspid-edge-to-edge-repair-in-r]]、[[来源/257-successful-ttvi-in-a-patient-with-rv-systolic-dy|257-successful-ttvi-in-a-patient-with-rv-systolic-dy]]

## 定义
经导管三尖瓣缘对缘修复（T-TEER）是一种通过导管将夹合装置（如 XTW/XT 系列器械）跨三尖瓣叶固定、从而减少三尖瓣反流的介入技术。临床实践中可根据解剖条件联合使用多枚器械，已成功用于 torrential 级别重度反流的治疗。该技术是目前经导管治疗三尖瓣反流的主要方式，EuroTR Score 和 GLIDE Score 均专为其术前风险分层与器械选择而开发。
