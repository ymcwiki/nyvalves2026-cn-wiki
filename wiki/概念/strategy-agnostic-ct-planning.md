# strategy agnostic CT planning

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/463-ct-should-be-the-cornerstone-in-as-case-based-de|463-ct-should-be-the-cornerstone-in-as-case-based-de]]

## 定义
主张 CT 规划不依附于已选 TAVR 或 SAVR，而应先于策略选择，为所有重度 AS 患者提供全面解剖评估，再由 heart team 决定治疗路径。
