# imaging modality-specific threshold

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/593-role-of-multimodality-imaging-in-assessment-of-a|593-role-of-multimodality-imaging-in-assessment-of-a]]

## 定义
不同影像模态（超声、CMR、CTA）对瓣膜反流严重程度的判定阈值各不相同，不能跨模态直接套用；ESC/EACTS 2025 指南明确强调这一原则。
