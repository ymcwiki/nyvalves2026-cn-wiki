# Monitored Anesthesia Care (MAC)

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/480-sedation-anesthesia-vs-general-endotracheal-for|480-sedation-anesthesia-vs-general-endotracheal-for]]、[[来源/604-beyond-tee-the-role-of-ice-in-contemporary-tricu|604-beyond-tee-the-role-of-ice-in-contemporary-tricu]]、[[来源/322-success-and-safety-of-transcatheter-tricuspid-va|322-success-and-safety-of-transcatheter-tricuspid-va]]

## 定义
Monitored Anesthesia Care，非气管插管的镇静麻醉方式，Piedmont ICE-only 流程中使用率达 90%，可减少血压波动和升压药使用。
