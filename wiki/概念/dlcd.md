# DLC/d

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/127-avoiding-basilica-in-redo-tavr-when-conventional|127-avoiding-basilica-in-redo-tavr-when-conventional]]、[[来源/145-ai-facilitated-operational-pathways-improving-ef|145-ai-facilitated-operational-pathways-improving-ef]]、[[来源/313-lifetime-management-of-aortic-disease-assisted-b|313-lifetime-management-of-aortic-disease-assisted-b]]、[[来源/667-materialise-mimics-for-cardiac-interventions-pla|667-materialise-mimics-for-cardiac-interventions-pla]]、[[来源/673-precision-planning-through-digital-twin-technolo|673-precision-planning-through-digital-twin-technolo]]、[[来源/658-subacute-thv-migration-after-unicorn-leaflet-mod|658-subacute-thv-migration-after-unicorn-leaflet-mod]]、[[来源/470-3d-simulation-guided-double-unicorn-viviv-tavr-w|470-3d-simulation-guided-double-unicorn-viviv-tavr-w]]

## 定义
DLC/d 是经导管主动脉瓣置换（TAVR）领域用于量化冠脉阻塞风险的无量纲比值，定义为瓣叶至冠脉开口距离（DLC）除以冠脉直径（d）。该指标常通过 DASI 等计算机断层模拟软件在术前计算，文献报道的风险阈值为 ≤0.7，基于超过 1500 例患者的前瞻性建模验证，灵敏度接近 100%。临床实践中，该比值同样适用于评估再次 TAVR（valve-in-valve）场景下的冠脉阻塞风险。
