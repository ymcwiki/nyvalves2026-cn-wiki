# RVFWLS

## 基本信息

- Source: [[来源/815-right-ventricular-performance-in-the-mitra-fr-tr|815-right-ventricular-performance-in-the-mitra-fr-tr]]、[[来源/805-ventricular-and-atrial-strain-analysis-after-m-t|805-ventricular-and-atrial-strain-analysis-after-m-t]]、[[来源/179-my-personal-guide-to-derisking-evoque-cases|179-my-personal-guide-to-derisking-evoque-cases]]

## 定义
右室游离壁纵向应变（right ventricular free wall longitudinal strain），与 PASP 之比（RVFWLS/PASP）用于评估右室功能，cut-off 0.50 具有预后判别价值。
