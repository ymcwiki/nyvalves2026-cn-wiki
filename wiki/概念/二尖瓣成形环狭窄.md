# 二尖瓣成形环狭窄

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/706-transcatheter-mitral-valve-in-ring-implantation|706-transcatheter-mitral-valve-in-ring-implantation]]

## 定义
外科二尖瓣成形环术后并发症，本病例中患者 MVA 降至0.8 cm²，跨瓣压差12–13 mmHg，为 TMViR 适应症。
