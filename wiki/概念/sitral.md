# SITRAL

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/412-prevent-first-a-decision-framework-for-lvot-prot|412-prevent-first-a-decision-framework-for-lvot-prot]]、[[来源/758-the-sitral-surgical-implantation-of-transcathete|758-the-sitral-surgical-implantation-of-transcathete]]、[[来源/853-transcatheter-valve-implantation-following-surgi|853-transcatheter-valve-implantation-following-surgi]]

## 定义
SITRAL（Surgical Implantation of TRAnscatheter vaLve / Surgical Implantation of Balloon-expandable valve）是针对重度二尖瓣环钙化（MAC）相关二尖瓣病变的外科手术策略：在直视下（可经机器人辅助或小切口开胸入路）切除前二尖瓣叶，随后植入球囊扩张式经导管瓣膜，必要时以毡垫（felt lining）加固瓣环。该术式适用于经导管或其他路径风险过高的重度 MAC 患者，也作为多学科评估中 NeoLVOT 间隙不足时的备选方案之一。
