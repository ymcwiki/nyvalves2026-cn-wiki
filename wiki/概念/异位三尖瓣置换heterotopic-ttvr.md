# 异位三尖瓣置换（Heterotopic TTVR）

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/360-heterotopic-ttvr-in-patients-with-severe-tricusp|360-heterotopic-ttvr-in-patients-with-severe-tricusp]]、[[来源/455-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-2-heterotopi|455-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-2-heterotopi]]、[[来源/365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve|365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve]]、[[来源/643-the-unica-heterotopic-cross-caval-ttvr-the-ottaw|643-the-unica-heterotopic-cross-caval-ttvr-the-ottaw]]

## 定义
在腔静脉-右房交界处而非三尖瓣原位植入人工瓣膜的经导管技术，适用于大瓣环、大对合间隙、RV功能下降等无法行原位置换的患者。
