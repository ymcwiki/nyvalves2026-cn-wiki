# HALT score

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/806-hypo-attenuated-leaflet-thickening-in-current-ba|806-hypo-attenuated-leaflet-thickening-in-current-ba]]

## 定义
对单个瓣叶HALT程度进行1-4分半定量评分，乘以受累瓣叶数量，总分0-12，用于量化BEV TAVI后HALT严重程度。
