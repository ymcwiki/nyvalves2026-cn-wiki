# 窦部隔绝（Sinus Sequestration）

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/651-real-world-outcomes-of-the-shortcut-device-for-l|651-real-world-outcomes-of-the-shortcut-device-for-l]]、[[来源/363-leaflet-avulsion-following-leaflet-splitting-dur|363-leaflet-avulsion-following-leaflet-splitting-dur]]、[[来源/408-my-practical-guide-to-tavr-with-obvious-or-poten|408-my-practical-guide-to-tavr-with-obvious-or-poten]]

## 定义
ViV TAVR 中旧瓣叶将冠状窦与主动脉腔隔离的解剖风险，伴或不伴直接冠脉开口阻塞，本研究中 56.7% 患者属于此类风险。
