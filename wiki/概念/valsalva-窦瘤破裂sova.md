# Valsalva 窦瘤破裂（SOVA）

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/694-in-vivo-electrosurgical-fenestration-of-mal-rota|694-in-vivo-electrosurgical-fenestration-of-mal-rota]]、[[来源/820-an-unexpected-turn-echocardiography-unmasks-annu|820-an-unexpected-turn-echocardiography-unmasks-annu]]

## 定义
Sinus of Valsalva Aneurysm 破裂，本例为右 Valsalva 窦破入右心房，形成约 7 mm 缺损并伴高速分流。
