# 心房功能性TR

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/489-efficacy-and-safety-of-combined-m-and-t-teer-wit|489-efficacy-and-safety-of-combined-m-and-t-teer-wit]]、[[来源/850-challenges-of-echocardiographic-assessment|850-challenges-of-echocardiographic-assessment]]、[[来源/477-when-the-clip-drops-the-beat-complete-heart-bloc|477-when-the-clip-drops-the-beat-complete-heart-bloc]]

## 定义
由心房扩大和瓣环扩张驱动的功能性三尖瓣反流，定义为tenting height≤10 mm、RV中部径≤38 mm、LV-EF≥50%，与非心房性TR预后有别。
