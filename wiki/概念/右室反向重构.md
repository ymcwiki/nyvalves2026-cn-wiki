## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve|365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve]]、[[来源/378-laplace-ttvr-system-6-months-efs-results|378-laplace-ttvr-system-6-months-efs-results]]、[[来源/636-imaging-parameters-beyond-tr-severity-what-else|636-imaging-parameters-beyond-tr-severity-what-else]]、[[来源/103-physiological-ongoing-rv-remodeling-with-vdyne-t|103-physiological-ongoing-rv-remodeling-with-vdyne-t]]、[[来源/566-case-resolution|566-case-resolution]]、[[来源/014-characterizing-clinical-success-after-ttvi-balan|014-characterizing-clinical-success-after-ttvi-balan]]

## 定义
TR 缓解后右室舒张末径和容积缩小、前向每搏量和心输出量改善的有利重塑过程，在 TRILUMINATE 和 TRISCEND II 均有记录。
