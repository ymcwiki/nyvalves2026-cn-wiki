# 中心静脉压（CVP）

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/360-heterotopic-ttvr-in-patients-with-severe-tricusp|360-heterotopic-ttvr-in-patients-with-severe-tricusp]]、[[来源/643-the-unica-heterotopic-cross-caval-ttvr-the-ottaw|643-the-unica-heterotopic-cross-caval-ttvr-the-ottaw]]

## 定义
衡量右心前负荷的血流动力学指标，Trillium入选标准要求peak CVP≥15 mmHg，植入后平均peak CVP从24降至18 mmHg。
