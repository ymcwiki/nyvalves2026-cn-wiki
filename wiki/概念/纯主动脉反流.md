## 基本信息

- Source: [[来源/218-treatment-of-pure-aortic-regurgitation-with-a-de|218-treatment-of-pure-aortic-regurgitation-with-a-de]]、[[来源/544-transcatheter-aortic-valve-replacement-for-pure|544-transcatheter-aortic-valve-replacement-for-pure]]、[[来源/759-reversing-regurgitant-physiology-pressure-volume|759-reversing-regurgitant-physiology-pressure-volume]]、[[来源/283-trilogy-transcatheter-aortic-valve-implantation|283-trilogy-transcatheter-aortic-valve-implantation]]、[[来源/750-physician-modified-repair-for-pure-native-severe|750-physician-modified-repair-for-pure-native-severe]]、[[来源/838-the-zeta-balloon-expandable-tavr-system-for-aort|838-the-zeta-balloon-expandable-tavr-system-for-aort]]

## 定义
不伴显著主动脉瓣狭窄的单纯主动脉反流，缺乏钙化锚定点导致常规 TAVR 固定困难，需专用瓣膜（如 JenaValve Trilogy）处理。
