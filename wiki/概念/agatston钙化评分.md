# Agatston钙化评分

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/124-challenging-case-1-balloon-expandable-valve|124-challenging-case-1-balloon-expandable-valve]]、[[来源/606-impact-of-transcatheter-aortic-valve-implantatio|606-impact-of-transcatheter-aortic-valve-implantatio]]、[[来源/612-from-float-to-fix-bailout-balloon-expandable-res|612-from-float-to-fix-bailout-balloon-expandable-res]]

## 定义
基于CT的钙化定量评分，用于评估主动脉瓣环钙化程度，本例评分482被认为属于低钙化，是TAVR锚定不足的主要危险因素之一。
