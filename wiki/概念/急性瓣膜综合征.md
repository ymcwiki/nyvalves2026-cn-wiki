# 急性瓣膜综合征

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/280-case-presentation-tavr-bioprosthetic-valve-failu|280-case-presentation-tavr-bioprosthetic-valve-failu]]、[[来源/288-optimal-surveillance-in-moderate-severe-as-pathw|288-optimal-surveillance-in-moderate-severe-as-pathw]]、[[来源/505-case-1-critical-aortic-stenosis-presenting-with|505-case-1-critical-aortic-stenosis-presenting-with]]、[[来源/380-balloon-aortic-valvuloplasty-bridging-improves-o|380-balloon-aortic-valvuloplasty-bridging-improves-o]]、[[来源/582-stable-progressive-and-acute-valve-syndrome-in-s|582-stable-progressive-and-acute-valve-syndrome-in-s]]

## 定义
急性瓣膜综合征指主动脉瓣疾病（包括主动脉瓣狭窄或 TAVR 术后瓣叶失效）进入急性失代偿阶段的临床现象，表现为急性重度主动脉反流、心源性休克或急性心力衰竭。EARLY TAVR 试验数据显示，约 40% 从保守治疗转为换瓣的无症状患者以急性或高级症状首诊，其 24 个月死亡/卒中率达 13.7%。进入心源性休克状态者预后更差，24 个月内死亡或心衰住院率高达 47.3%，是主动脉瓣疾病管理中需重点预防的高危临床表型。
