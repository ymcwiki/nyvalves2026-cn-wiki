# TAPSE/PASP

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/346-ttvi-indications-options-outcomes-best-practice|346-ttvi-indications-options-outcomes-best-practice]]、[[来源/689-prognostic-value-of-right-ventricular-pulmonary|689-prognostic-value-of-right-ventricular-pulmonary]]、[[来源/799-implications-of-the-tapse-pasp-ratio-in-mitral-v|799-implications-of-the-tapse-pasp-ratio-in-mitral-v]]、[[来源/567-heterotopic-tricuspid-valve-replacement-more-tha|567-heterotopic-tricuspid-valve-replacement-more-tha]]、[[来源/815-right-ventricular-performance-in-the-mitra-fr-tr|815-right-ventricular-performance-in-the-mitra-fr-tr]]、[[来源/515-atrial-secondary-tr-assessment-and-management-ch|515-atrial-secondary-tr-assessment-and-management-ch]]

## 定义
三尖瓣环平面收缩期位移与肺动脉收缩压之比，反映右室-肺动脉耦联，High TAPSE/PASP 组全因死亡率显著低于 Low 组（log-rank P=0.042）。
