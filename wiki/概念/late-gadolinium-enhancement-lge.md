# late gadolinium enhancement (LGE)

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/590-exceptionally-rare-anterolateral-papillary-muscl|590-exceptionally-rare-anterolateral-papillary-muscl]]、[[来源/011-recurrent-minoca-after-temporary-dapt-interrupti|011-recurrent-minoca-after-temporary-dapt-interrupti]]、[[来源/451-acute-aortic-valve-syndrome-my-personal-checklis|451-acute-aortic-valve-syndrome-my-personal-checklis]]

## 定义
CMR 钆对比剂延迟强化技术，本病例中显示仅少量周边 LGE、无中心强化，有助于排除恶性病变并支持干酪样坏死诊断。
