# 乳头肌断裂

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/160-flail-to-fix-challenging-transcatheter-tricuspid|160-flail-to-fix-challenging-transcatheter-tricuspid]]、[[来源/231-the-spectrum-and-treatment-algorithms-for-acute|231-the-spectrum-and-treatment-algorithms-for-acute]]、[[来源/492-m-teer-in-the-setting-of-cardiogenic-shock-requi|492-m-teer-in-the-setting-of-cardiogenic-shock-requi]]、[[来源/509-ecmo-assisted-mv-teer-in-setting-of-acute-papill|509-ecmo-assisted-mv-teer-in-setting-of-acute-papill]]、[[来源/514-the-spectrum-and-treatment-algorithms-for-acute|514-the-spectrum-and-treatment-algorithms-for-acute]]

## 定义
乳头肌断裂是心肌梗死后罕见但致命的机械并发症，在STEMI患者中发生率约为0.05%至0.26%，以下壁心梗累及后内乳头肌最为典型。断裂发生后，二尖瓣失去腱索支撑，出现瓣叶连枷运动，引发急性重度二尖瓣反流乃至洪水样反流，迅速进展为心源性休克（SCAI D级）。早期TEE是识别该并发症的关键手段，断裂的乳头肌残端在某些介入操作中也可作为器械锚定的解剖结构加以利用。
