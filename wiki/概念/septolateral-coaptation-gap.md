# Septolateral coaptation gap

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/811-minimizing-complications-of-tteer-my-personal-ch|811-minimizing-complications-of-tteer-my-personal-ch]]、[[来源/813-anatomical-and-clinical-factors-affecting-treatm|813-anatomical-and-clinical-factors-affecting-treatm]]、[[来源/346-ttvi-indications-options-outcomes-best-practice|346-ttvi-indications-options-outcomes-best-practice]]

## 定义
T-TEER 解剖适宜性的核心指标，间隔-外侧方向间距，≤7 mm 为有利解剖，>10-15 mm 为不利解剖，直接影响修复还是置换的选择。
