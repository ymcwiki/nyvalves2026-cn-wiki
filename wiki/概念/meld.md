# MELD

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/567-heterotopic-tricuspid-valve-replacement-more-tha|567-heterotopic-tricuspid-valve-replacement-more-tha]]、[[来源/717-sts-tricuspid-score-validation-and-risk-factors|717-sts-tricuspid-score-validation-and-risk-factors]]

## 定义
终末期肝病模型评分，用于捕捉非心脏风险；MELD-XI ≥14 与 TTVI 后更差 1 年结局相关，是无效治疗的重要边界。
