# ICD（inter-commissural distance）

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/653-case-3-risk-of-aortic-root-rupture-in-bicuspid-a|653-case-3-risk-of-aortic-root-rupture-in-bicuspid-a]]、[[来源/537-challenging-thv-sizing-in-bicuspid-aortic-valve|537-challenging-thv-sizing-in-bicuspid-aortic-valve]]、[[来源/561-treatment-of-bicuspid-aortic-valve-disease-a-sta|561-treatment-of-bicuspid-aortic-valve-disease-a-sta]]

## 定义
连合间距，即 +4 mm 平面的连合部间距，是 ABC 算法中评估二叶式主动脉瓣根部解剖空间余量的关键指标，ICD-valve 直径 <-1 mm 为高危标准。
