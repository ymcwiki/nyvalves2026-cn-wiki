# 瓣叶tethering

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/061-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-2-transcathe|061-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-2-transcathe]]

## 定义
瓣叶被腱索或乳头肌束缚向下牵拉，导致对合不良，本例tethering超过10mm并靠近乳头肌，增加TTVR难度。
