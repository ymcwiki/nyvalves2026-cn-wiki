# stent frame under-expansion

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/631-case-1-acute-complications-of-incomplete-transca|631-case-1-acute-complications-of-incomplete-transca]]

## 定义
TAVR 支架框架未能充分扩张至预期形态，轻度时影响耐久性，严重时可造成急性血流动力学崩溃。
