# true internal diameter

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/037-impact-of-true-id-and-valve-fracture-on-gradient|037-impact-of-true-id-and-valve-fracture-on-gradient]]、[[来源/483-how-do-i-prepare-for-tricuspid-valve-in-x-proced|483-how-do-i-prepare-for-tricuspid-valve-in-x-proced]]、[[来源/724-ct-planning-for-both-surgical-and-tavr-valve-in|724-ct-planning-for-both-surgical-and-tavr-valve-in]]、[[来源/137-how-do-i-prepare-for-aortic-valve-in-valve-proce|137-how-do-i-prepare-for-aortic-valve-in-valve-proce]]

## 定义
外科生物瓣的实际内径，区别于厂商标称号数，反映瓣膜内部真实可用空间。它是瓣中瓣（ViV）TAVI 及三尖瓣 ViV sizing 的关键参数：≤21 mm 与术后更高的 1 年残余梯度相关，临床上需通过植入卡或手术记录查阅确认。
