# 冠脉遮挡

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/311-how-to-step-by-step-case-in-a-box-and-simulator|311-how-to-step-by-step-case-in-a-box-and-simulator]]、[[来源/470-3d-simulation-guided-double-unicorn-viviv-tavr-w|470-3d-simulation-guided-double-unicorn-viviv-tavr-w]]

## 定义
TAVR时原有瓣叶被推向冠脉开口造成血流阻塞的并发症，BASILICA技术通过预先裂开瓣叶来降低此风险。
