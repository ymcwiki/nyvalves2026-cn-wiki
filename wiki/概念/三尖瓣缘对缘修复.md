# 三尖瓣缘对缘修复

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/245-complex-transcatheter-tricuspid-valve-replacemen|245-complex-transcatheter-tricuspid-valve-replacemen]]、[[来源/822-transcatheter-tricuspid-valve-annuloplasty-versu|822-transcatheter-tricuspid-valve-annuloplasty-versu]]、[[来源/834-case-presentation-1-moderate-severe-tr-with-symp|834-case-presentation-1-moderate-severe-tr-with-symp]]、[[来源/061-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-2-transcathe|061-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-2-transcathe]]、[[来源/063-don-t-be-baffled-tricuspid-transcatheter-valve-e|063-don-t-be-baffled-tricuspid-transcatheter-valve-e]]

## 定义
经导管三尖瓣修复技术，使用夹合器（如 TriClip）夹合三尖瓣瓣叶，本例因中央夹合附着不理想导致双孔解剖和复发重度 TR。
