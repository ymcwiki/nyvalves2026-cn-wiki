# EuroTR score

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/339-keynote-lecture-knowledge-practice-and-research|339-keynote-lecture-knowledge-practice-and-research]]、[[来源/834-case-presentation-1-moderate-severe-tr-with-symp|834-case-presentation-1-moderate-severe-tr-with-symp]]、[[来源/842-do-we-need-more-objective-risk-scores|842-do-we-need-more-objective-risk-scores]]、[[来源/135-t-varc-2-guidelines-new-tricuspid-therapies|135-t-varc-2-guidelines-new-tricuspid-therapies]]

## 定义
EuroTR score 是专为三尖瓣干预（TTVI/T-TEER）设计的风险评估工具，整合左室射血分数、TAPSE、eGFR、NT-proBNP等临床、超声心动图、血流动力学及实验室指标，对术后死亡风险和不良临床结局进行量化预测。评分按区间划分早中晚期疾病阶段，与 1 年死亡率线性相关，预测 AUC 达 0.763–0.838，优于现有主要评分系统。临床实例中评分高达 59.9% 时提示患者处于极高外科手术风险。
