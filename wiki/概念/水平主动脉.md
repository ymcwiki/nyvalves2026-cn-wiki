# 水平主动脉

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/104-effect-of-a-horizontal-aorta-on-procedural-and-c|104-effect-of-a-horizontal-aorta-on-procedural-and-c]]、[[来源/366-feasibility-and-safety-of-ebu-catheter-assisted|366-feasibility-and-safety-of-ebu-catheter-assisted]]、[[来源/497-tav-in-tav-in-complex-anatomy-with-horizontal-ro|497-tav-in-tav-in-complex-anatomy-with-horizontal-ro]]

## 定义
以主动脉角 ≥48° 定义的解剖变异，既往认为与 TAVR 程序成功率低、PVL 和 PPI 风险高相关，本研究显示当代新一代 THV 可能削弱其不利影响。
