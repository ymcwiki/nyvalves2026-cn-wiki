# RLM

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/026-advanced-imaging-ct-cmr-after-ttvi-assessing-res|026-advanced-imaging-ct-cmr-after-ttvi-assessing-res]]、[[来源/740-halt-prevention-and-treatment-pharmacology-and-b|740-halt-prevention-and-treatment-pharmacology-and-b]]、[[来源/778-the-halt-imaging-playbook-definitions-and-case-e|778-the-halt-imaging-playbook-definitions-and-case-e]]、[[来源/800-outcomes-and-management-of-hypoattenuating-leafl|800-outcomes-and-management-of-hypoattenuating-leafl]]

## 定义
RLM（瓣叶活动度降低／reduced leaflet motion）指人工瓣膜置换后出现的瓣叶运动幅度减小现象，与HALT（瓣叶血栓性增厚）密切相关，两者并存时提示瓣膜血栓形成。RLM可导致跨瓣压差显著升高，TTE对其检出不敏感，CT是早期诊断的重要手段，经抗凝治疗后通常可逆转。
