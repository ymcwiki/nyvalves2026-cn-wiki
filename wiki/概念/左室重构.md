# 左室重构

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/286-jenavalve-trilogy-transcatheter-aortic-valve-imp|286-jenavalve-trilogy-transcatheter-aortic-valve-imp]]、[[来源/577-the-landscape-for-transcatheter-valve-therapy-fo|577-the-landscape-for-transcatheter-valve-therapy-fo]]、[[来源/654-using-a-coronary-sinus-approach-carillon-updates|654-using-a-coronary-sinus-approach-carillon-updates]]

## 定义
主动脉瓣置换后左室内径（LVEDD、LVESD）和容积（LVEDV、LVESV）随时间缩小的过程，是评估瓣膜治疗效果的重要超声指标，ALIGN-AR和本地队列均显示正向重构。
