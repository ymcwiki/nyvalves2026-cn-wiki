# 功能性三尖瓣反流

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/246-staged-mitraclip-and-trillium-cross-caval-valve|246-staged-mitraclip-and-trillium-cross-caval-valve]]、[[来源/365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve|365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve]]、[[来源/355-scope-of-the-problem-and-clinical-recognition|355-scope-of-the-problem-and-clinical-recognition]]

## 定义
继发于右室扩张和三尖瓣环扩大的三尖瓣反流，本例为倾盆样程度，巨大对合间隙使 T-TEER 不可行，严重右室扩张也排除了常规 TTVR。
