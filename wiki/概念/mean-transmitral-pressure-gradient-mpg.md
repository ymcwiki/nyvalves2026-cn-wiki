# mean transmitral pressure gradient (MPG)

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/273-optimizing-procedural-m-teer-outcomes-balancing|273-optimizing-procedural-m-teer-outcomes-balancing]]、[[来源/775-early-outcomes-of-transcatheter-edge-to-edge-rep|775-early-outcomes-of-transcatheter-edge-to-edge-rep]]

## 定义
M-TEER 后评估医源性二尖瓣狭窄的常用替代指标，阈值为 5 mmHg，但在多变量模型中预后信号弱于 rMR。
