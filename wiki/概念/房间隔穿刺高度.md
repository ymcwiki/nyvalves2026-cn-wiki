# 房间隔穿刺高度

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/229-bailout-of-entrapped-mitraclip-using-a-second-tr|229-bailout-of-entrapped-mitraclip-using-a-second-tr]]、[[来源/371-revalve-solutions-beyond-the-limits-of-teer-and|371-revalve-solutions-beyond-the-limits-of-teer-and]]

## 定义
TEER 手术中穿刺房间隔时的位置高度，对 clip 的操控自由度和 straddle 质量有决定性影响，本例中约 2.5 cm 偏低。
