# 假体瓣感染性心内膜炎

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/546-complex-biological-prosthesis-disease-how-to-rep|546-complex-biological-prosthesis-disease-how-to-rep]]、[[来源/001-prosthetic-valve-endocarditis-following-surgical|001-prosthetic-valve-endocarditis-following-surgical]]

## 定义
Prosthetic Valve Endocarditis（PVE），本病例为假体下感染性心内膜炎（SAMS），并发瓣环脓肿，经内科治疗后转入经皮修复评估。
