# 主动脉根部扩大

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/106-complex-transcatheter-aortic-valve-interventions|106-complex-transcatheter-aortic-valve-interventions]]、[[来源/323-case-presentation-asymptomatic-severe-as|323-case-presentation-asymptomatic-severe-as]]、[[来源/841-the-reality-of-root-enlargement-not-for-everyone|841-the-reality-of-root-enlargement-not-for-everyone]]

## 定义
通过 Nicks、Manouguian 或 Konno 等外科技术扩大主动脉根部，在小瓣环患者中植入更大假体、消除 PPM 并保留未来再干预选项。
