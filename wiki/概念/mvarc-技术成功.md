# MVARC 技术成功

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/682-m-teer-for-patients-with-a-small-mitral-valve-or|682-m-teer-for-patients-with-a-small-mitral-valve-or]]、[[来源/733-featured-lecture-real-world-outcomes-of-transsep|733-featured-lecture-real-world-outcomes-of-transsep]]、[[来源/775-early-outcomes-of-transcatheter-edge-to-edge-rep|775-early-outcomes-of-transcatheter-edge-to-edge-rep]]、[[来源/538-smart-navigation-robotic-delivery-of-luna-tmvr-a|538-smart-navigation-robotic-delivery-of-luna-tmvr-a]]

## 定义
MVARC（二尖瓣学术研究联盟）制定的程序成功评估标准，用于经导管二尖瓣手术的主要终点定义。具体标准包括：无操作相关死亡、成功完成入路输送及器械回收、首个计划器械正确定位、无急诊手术或再干预。多项临床研究（MIRACLE 等）以此为主要终点，报告技术成功率在 92%–96% 区间。
