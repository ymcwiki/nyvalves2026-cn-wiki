# Virchow三联征

## 基本信息
- Type: theory
- Source: [[来源/024-thrombus-in-the-shadow-of-the-valve-a-case-of-po|024-thrombus-in-the-shadow-of-the-valve-a-case-of-po]]、[[来源/755-the-central-controversy-does-halt-have-clinical|755-the-central-controversy-does-halt-have-clinical]]、[[来源/746-predictors-of-halt-and-the-role-of-frame-expansi|746-predictors-of-halt-and-the-role-of-frame-expansi]]

## 定义
Virchow三联征是解释血栓形成机制的经典理论，核心三要素为血流停滞、内皮损伤和高凝状态。该理论被改良应用于TAVR及瓣中瓣（ViV TAVR）术后场景，用于阐明新窦内血栓形成的病理机制及患者血栓风险的来源。
