# 腔静脉瓣膜介入（CAVI）

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/114-transcatheter-caval-valve-trials-case-study-tric|114-transcatheter-caval-valve-trials-case-study-tric]]、[[来源/136-pros-and-cons-of-orthotopic-and-hetertopic-ttvr|136-pros-and-cons-of-orthotopic-and-hetertopic-ttvr]]、[[来源/365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve|365-cross-caval-valve-replacement-with-the-tricvalve]]、[[来源/567-heterotopic-tricuspid-valve-replacement-more-tha|567-heterotopic-tricuspid-valve-replacement-more-tha]]

## 定义
在上下腔静脉放置有功能瓣膜以减少 TR 反流回灌的介入策略，不依赖三尖瓣解剖，保留未来TV治疗选项，TricValve 是其代表装置。
