# 三尖瓣反流（TR）

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/125-the-ttvr-track-record-30-day-real-world-validati|125-the-ttvr-track-record-30-day-real-world-validati]]、[[来源/322-success-and-safety-of-transcatheter-tricuspid-va|322-success-and-safety-of-transcatheter-tricuspid-va]]、[[来源/355-scope-of-the-problem-and-clinical-recognition|355-scope-of-the-problem-and-clinical-recognition]]、[[来源/173-the-epidemiologic-landscape-of-valvular-heart-di|173-the-epidemiologic-landscape-of-valvular-heart-di]]、[[来源/458-case-presentation-2-ttvr-in-the-setting-of-rv-dy|458-case-presentation-2-ttvr-in-the-setting-of-rv-dy]]、[[来源/384-lux-valve-plus-ttvr-system-12-month-outcomes-in|384-lux-valve-plus-ttvr-system-12-month-outcomes-in]]

## 定义
三尖瓣反流（TR）是三尖瓣关闭不全导致收缩期血液从右室反流入右房的瓣膜病变，美国患病率约3.7%，是最常见却治疗率极低的瓣膜疾病之一。右房容量负荷长期增加可进展为右心衰，严重TR与高死亡率显著相关。经导管介入治疗30天随访数据显示，96%患者TR可降至mild或以下，提示新型微创术式具有确切的血流动力学疗效。
