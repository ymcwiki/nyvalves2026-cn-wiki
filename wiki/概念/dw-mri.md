# DW-MRI

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/296-emboliner-total-body-embolic-protection-system-e|296-emboliner-total-body-embolic-protection-system-e]]、[[来源/289-emblok-embolic-protection-device-innovative-card|289-emblok-embolic-protection-device-innovative-card]]、[[来源/284-point-guard-cerebral-embolic-protection-transver|284-point-guard-cerebral-embolic-protection-transver]]、[[来源/308-protembo-complete-cerebral-embolic-protection-sy|308-protembo-complete-cerebral-embolic-protection-sy]]

## 定义
弥散加权磁共振成像，用于检测TAVR后缺血性脑损伤，研究显示多数TAVR患者存在DW-MRI可见的脑损伤（68–93%）。
