# PASS 标准

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/185-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-how-mini-tee|185-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-how-mini-tee]]、[[来源/729-imaging-guidance-for-concomitant-af-ablation-and|729-imaging-guidance-for-concomitant-af-ablation-and]]、[[来源/787-laac-tavr-with-case-examples|787-laac-tavr-with-case-examples]]、[[来源/171-balancing-quality-and-efficiency-selecting-the-o|171-balancing-quality-and-efficiency-selecting-the-o]]、[[来源/307-procedural-steps-made-simple-trans-septal-access|307-procedural-steps-made-simple-trans-septal-access]]、[[来源/123-navigating-difficult-anatomy-shallow-appendages|123-navigating-difficult-anatomy-shallow-appendages]]

## 定义
PASS 标准是左心耳封堵器（如 Watchman）植入后评估释放是否合格的超声评估框架，涵盖四个维度：Position（位置是否适当）、Anchor（锚定是否稳固）、Size（压缩比是否在 10%–30% 范围内）、Seal（封堵是否严密、无残余漏）。四项标准须全部满足，方可最终释放器械。
