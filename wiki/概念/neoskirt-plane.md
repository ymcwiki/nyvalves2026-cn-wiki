# Neoskirt plane

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/170-rapid-ventricular-pacing-to-optimize-ttvr-device|170-rapid-ventricular-pacing-to-optimize-ttvr-device]]、[[来源/172-ct-analysis|172-ct-analysis]]、[[来源/724-ct-planning-for-both-surgical-and-tavr-valve-in|724-ct-planning-for-both-surgical-and-tavr-valve-in]]、[[来源/030-real-time-fluoroscopic-assessment-of-left-main-o|030-real-time-fluoroscopic-assessment-of-left-main-o]]、[[来源/691-impact-of-frame-architecture-in-redo-tavr-tall-a|691-impact-of-frame-architecture-in-redo-tavr-tall-a]]

## 定义
Neoskirt plane 是经导管瓣膜置入（TAVR 及 Valve-in-Valve/TAV-in-TAV）术后形成的关键解剖参考平面。在 EVOQUE 三尖瓣置换中，该平面由瓣架与三尖瓣环交界共同构成；在主动脉瓣 Valve-in-Valve 场景中，则由固定的旧瓣叶与新置入 TAV 内裙（neoskirt）顶端所定义的管状结构顶部平面。该平面是评估器械轴向定位的核心基准，同时决定冠脉阻塞风险与瓣叶悬挂（leaflet overhang）风险之间的平衡点。
