# 压力恢复

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/542-non-invasive-pressure-recovery-and-echo-invasive|542-non-invasive-pressure-recovery-and-echo-invasive]]、[[来源/851-hemodynamics-and-flow-patterns-in-small-annuli|851-hemodynamics-and-flow-patterns-in-small-annuli]]

## 定义
血流经过狭窄孔口后在升主动脉远端减速，动能重新转化为压力的物理现象，导致 Doppler 超声测量值系统性高于有创净跨瓣压差。
