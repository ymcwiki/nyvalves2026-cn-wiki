# ALARA

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/261-recurrent-mr-after-m-teer-how-failure-modes-guid|261-recurrent-mr-after-m-teer-how-failure-modes-guid]]

## 定义
As Low As Reasonably Achievable，本文用于描述M-TEER index手术的目标：残余MR应尽量最低，以减少后续复发风险。
