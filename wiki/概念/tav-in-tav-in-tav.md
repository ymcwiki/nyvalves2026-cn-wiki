# TAV-in-TAV-in-TAV

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/220-tav-in-tav-in-tav-for-failed-low-valve-implantat|220-tav-in-tav-in-tav-for-failed-low-valve-implantat]]、[[来源/304-triple-transcatheter-aortic-valve-replacement-va|304-triple-transcatheter-aortic-valve-replacement-va]]、[[来源/032-management-of-balloon-rupture-during-valve-in-va|032-management-of-balloon-rupture-during-valve-in-va]]

## 定义
在两层既有经导管主动脉瓣框架内再植入第三枚瓣膜的罕见补救策略，本例用26 mm S3 按70/30深度释放并高压后扩，成功消除重度 PVL。
