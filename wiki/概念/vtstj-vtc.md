# VTSTJ / VTC

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/796-to-basilica-or-not-to-basilica-a-technical-pivot|796-to-basilica-or-not-to-basilica-a-technical-pivot]]、[[来源/485-simplifying-leaflet-laceration-pi-cardia-shortcu|485-simplifying-leaflet-laceration-pi-cardia-shortcu]]、[[来源/137-how-do-i-prepare-for-aortic-valve-in-valve-proce|137-how-do-i-prepare-for-aortic-valve-in-valve-proce]]、[[来源/337-making-the-case-for-aggressive-leaflet-modificat|337-making-the-case-for-aggressive-leaflet-modificat]]

## 定义
CT 规划中测量虚拟瓣膜与窦管交界区（VTSTJ）及冠脉开口（VTC）的距离，用于量化 TAVR 后冠脉阻塞风险，本例数值极低（VTSTJ 约 2.6 mm，VTC 3.2-3.9 mm）。
