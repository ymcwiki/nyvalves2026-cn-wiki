# 前二尖瓣叶

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/219-how-to-step-by-step-case-in-a-box-and-simulator|219-how-to-step-by-step-case-in-a-box-and-simulator]]、[[来源/765-gotham-in-the-cath-lab-batman-facilitated-tmvr-v|765-gotham-in-the-cath-lab-batman-facilitated-tmvr-v]]

## 定义
LAMPOON 手术的切割靶点，前二尖瓣叶在 TMVR 后若向 LVOT 移位可造成流出道阻塞，裂开后可消除此风险。
