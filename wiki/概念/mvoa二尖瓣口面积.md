# MVOA（二尖瓣口面积）

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/252-making-the-case-for-catheter-based-intervention|252-making-the-case-for-catheter-based-intervention]]、[[来源/414-case-resolutions|414-case-resolutions]]、[[来源/682-m-teer-for-patients-with-a-small-mitral-valve-or|682-m-teer-for-patients-with-a-small-mitral-valve-or]]

## 定义
评估 redo M-TEER 解剖可行性的关键参数，标准路径要求 ≥3.5 cm²，非中央目标可接受 ≥3.0 cm²。
