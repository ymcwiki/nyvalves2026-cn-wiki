# CT 主动脉瓣钙化评分

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/790-the-diagnostic-pitfalls-of-multi-valve-disease|790-the-diagnostic-pitfalls-of-multi-valve-disease]]、[[来源/428-guideline-recommendation|428-guideline-recommendation]]、[[来源/473-case-presentation-tri-leaflet-aortic-valve-steno|473-case-presentation-tri-leaflet-aortic-valve-steno]]

## 定义
通过 CT 定量测量主动脉瓣钙化体积，用于低流量 AS 的严重程度确认，男性阈值 ≥2000 AU，女性 ≥1200 AU。
