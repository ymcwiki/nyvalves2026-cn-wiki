# CMR

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/855-tavr-in-aortic-insufficiency-look-beyond-the-val|855-tavr-in-aortic-insufficiency-look-beyond-the-val]]、[[来源/343-transcatheter-retrieval-of-a-damaged-mitraclip-a|343-transcatheter-retrieval-of-a-damaged-mitraclip-a]]

## 定义
心脏磁共振（Cardiac Magnetic Resonance），在AR评估中LVESVi和主动脉反流分数（ARF）的CMR测量比超声更能预测临床结局，是优于常规超声的影像学工具。
