# 左室假性动脉瘤

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/169-transcatheter-closure-of-a-complex-left-ventricu|169-transcatheter-closure-of-a-complex-left-ventricu]]、[[来源/357-percutaneous-retrograde-transaortic-closure-of-a|357-percutaneous-retrograde-transaortic-closure-of-a]]

## 定义
心室壁破裂后由心包或瘢痕组织包绕形成的假腔，本例位于左室基底内侧后壁，与二尖瓣PVL相通。
