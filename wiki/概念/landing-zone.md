# Landing zone

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/123-navigating-difficult-anatomy-shallow-appendages|123-navigating-difficult-anatomy-shallow-appendages]]、[[来源/215-tee-and-cta-case-examples-for-laac-pre-procedura|215-tee-and-cta-case-examples-for-laac-pre-procedura]]、[[来源/336-case-1-watchman-ice-guidance-live-case-in-a-box|336-case-1-watchman-ice-guidance-live-case-in-a-box]]

## 定义
Landing zone 指左心耳封堵器在LAA内预期锚定并形成密封的区域，通常位于口部（ostium）以远，其可用深度和直径共同决定器械类型与规格的选择。该区域的尺寸通常以距口部一定深度处的直径为准，临床上据此匹配对应封堵器型号（如landing zone 13–14 mm对应18 mm Amulet）。当LAA解剖较浅时，landing zone深度不足，成为器械选择的核心挑战。
