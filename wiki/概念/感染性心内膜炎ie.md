# 感染性心内膜炎（IE）

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/385-five-year-risk-of-infective-endocarditis-followi|385-five-year-risk-of-infective-endocarditis-followi]]、[[来源/609-percutaneous-and-medical-management-of-harmony-t|609-percutaneous-and-medical-management-of-harmony-t]]、[[来源/154-complex-paravalvular-leak-closure-in-a-rocking-m|154-complex-paravalvular-leak-closure-in-a-rocking-m]]、[[来源/576-device-driven-endocardial-failure-retained-chemo|576-device-driven-endocardial-failure-retained-chemo]]

## 定义
人工瓣膜植入后的严重并发症，死亡率高；本研究以 5 年累积 IE 发生率为主要终点比较 TAVR 与 SAVR 术后风险。
