# 锚定环（anchor ring）设计

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/255-transfemoral-j-valve-system-for-chronic-aortic-r|255-transfemoral-j-valve-system-for-chronic-aortic-r]]、[[来源/821-transcatheter-treatment-of-chronic-aortic-regurg|821-transcatheter-treatment-of-chronic-aortic-regurg]]

## 定义
J-Valve TF 独特的锚定机制，将锚定环展开至主动脉窦内以提供径向支撑，解决纯 AR 缺乏钙化锚定区的难题。
