# 残余漏

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/285-laac-procedure-and-device-related-complications|285-laac-procedure-and-device-related-complications]]

## 定义
LAAO装置与左心耳壁之间残余血流交通，NCDR数据显示任意漏发生率26.6%，多为小漏，小PDL与卒中/TIA/SE风险HR 1.15相关。
