# Cardiohepatic Syndrome

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/479-hemodynamics-optimization-before-ttvi-heart-team|479-hemodynamics-optimization-before-ttvi-heart-team]]、[[来源/112-impact-of-transcatheter-tricuspid-valve-replacem|112-impact-of-transcatheter-tricuspid-valve-replacem]]

## 定义
心肝综合征，定义为碱性磷酸酶、γ-谷氨酰转移酶、胆红素三项胆汁淤积指标中至少2项升高，是 T-TEER 后死亡和心衰住院的重要预测因子。
