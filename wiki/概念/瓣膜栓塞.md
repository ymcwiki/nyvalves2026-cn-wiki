# 瓣膜栓塞

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/348-transapical-retrieval-of-an-embolized-valve-duri|348-transapical-retrieval-of-an-embolized-valve-duri]]、[[来源/827-tav-in-tav-through-a-previuosly-embolized-self-e|827-tav-in-tav-through-a-previuosly-embolized-self-e]]、[[来源/577-the-landscape-for-transcatheter-valve-therapy-fo|577-the-landscape-for-transcatheter-valve-therapy-fo]]

## 定义
在AR-TAVR中，因缺少钙化锚定和高每搏量，经导管心脏瓣膜可能发生位置迁移或栓塞，PANTHEON研究显示总发生率12.4%，后扩张为其预测因素。
