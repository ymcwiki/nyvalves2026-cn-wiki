# unfractionated heparin

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/625-unfractionated-heparin-timing-in-transseptal-pun|625-unfractionated-heparin-timing-in-transseptal-pun]]

## 定义
未分馏肝素，用于TSP相关手术中降低血栓栓塞风险，TSP前给予UFH可将鞘管相关血栓从15.4%降至0%，但可能加重心包积液风险。
