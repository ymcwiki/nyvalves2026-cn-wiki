# 低流量低梯度主动脉瓣狭窄（LFLG AS）

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/551-anatomic-hemodynamic-discordance-in-low-flow-low|551-anatomic-hemodynamic-discordance-in-low-flow-low]]、[[来源/118-challenging-case-2-self-expanding-valve|118-challenging-case-2-self-expanding-valve]]、[[来源/505-case-1-critical-aortic-stenosis-presenting-with|505-case-1-critical-aortic-stenosis-presenting-with]]、[[来源/790-the-diagnostic-pitfalls-of-multi-valve-disease|790-the-diagnostic-pitfalls-of-multi-valve-disease]]、[[来源/754-case-resolution|754-case-resolution]]

## 定义
定义为AVA≤1 cm²、平均跨瓣压差MG<40 mmHg、每搏量指数SVi≤35 mL/m²的主动脉瓣狭窄亚型，其真正严重程度判断具有挑战性，需结合解剖和血流动力学多种评估手段。
