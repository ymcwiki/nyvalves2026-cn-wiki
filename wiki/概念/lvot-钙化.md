# LVOT 钙化

## 基本信息
- Type: term
- Source: [[来源/106-complex-transcatheter-aortic-valve-interventions|106-complex-transcatheter-aortic-valve-interventions]]、[[来源/820-an-unexpected-turn-echocardiography-unmasks-annu|820-an-unexpected-turn-echocardiography-unmasks-annu]]、[[来源/199-the-use-of-multi-modality-imaging-for-percutaneo|199-the-use-of-multi-modality-imaging-for-percutaneo]]

## 定义
左室流出道钙化，严重时使 TAVR 瓣环破裂风险增加 7.5 倍（院内死亡率 50%），本讲认为 SAVR 直接去钙化是唯一确定性解决方案。
