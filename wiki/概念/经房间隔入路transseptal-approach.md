# 经房间隔入路（transseptal approach）

## 基本信息
- Type: method
- Source: [[来源/178-a-primer-on-mitral-pvl-closure-assessment-planni|178-a-primer-on-mitral-pvl-closure-assessment-planni]]、[[来源/389-transcatheter-transseptal-mitral-valve-saturn-im|389-transcatheter-transseptal-mitral-valve-saturn-im]]、[[来源/169-transcatheter-closure-of-a-complex-left-ventricu|169-transcatheter-closure-of-a-complex-left-ventricu]]、[[来源/781-altavalve-tmvr-in-mac-procedural-and-clinical-re|781-altavalve-tmvr-in-mac-procedural-and-clinical-re]]

## 定义
二尖瓣 PVL 封堵的首选路径，配合 14F 大可调弯鞘获得同轴对准，技术成功率 93.5%，优于既往 72.7%，手术时间也从 64 分钟缩短至 28.5 分钟。
