# NCD 20.34

## 基本信息
- Type: standard
- Source: [[来源/156-will-option-limit-laac-expansion-to-ambulatory-s|156-will-option-limit-laac-expansion-to-ambulatory-s]]、[[来源/177-launching-a-new-laac-program-from-dust-to-dollar|177-launching-a-new-laac-program-from-dust-to-dollar]]、[[来源/768-reimbursement-for-categories-a-and-b-what-cms-lo|768-reimbursement-for-categories-a-and-b-what-cms-lo]]、[[来源/148-developing-a-high-functioning-heart-valve-team-a|148-developing-a-high-functioning-heart-valve-team-a]]、[[来源/354-opening-remarks-tavr-dissemination-through-the-n|354-opening-remarks-tavr-dissemination-through-the-n]]

## 定义
CMS 关于 LAAC 的全国覆盖决策，要求手术必须在拥有既定结构性心脏病项目和/或电生理项目的医院完成，并参与注册登记，是 LAAC 无法进入 ASC 的核心监管障碍。
