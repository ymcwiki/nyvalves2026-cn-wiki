# THV 欠扩张

## 基本信息
- Type: phenomenon
- Source: [[来源/681-effect-of-nominal-volume-double-tap-post-dilatat|681-effect-of-nominal-volume-double-tap-post-dilatat]]、[[来源/513-lost-in-translation-why-engineers-need-a-seat-at|513-lost-in-translation-why-engineers-need-a-seat-at]]、[[来源/537-challenging-thv-sizing-in-bicuspid-aortic-valve|537-challenging-thv-sizing-in-bicuspid-aortic-valve]]

## 定义
TAVR 术后瓣架未能达到标称尺寸扩张的状态，本研究以 mid-stent frame 腰部为重点，≥20% 定义为显著欠扩张，与 HVD 风险升高相关。
