# Amulet 360

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/238-device-characteristics-clinical-data-updates-and|238-device-characteristics-clinical-data-updates-and]]、[[来源/429-impact-of-procedural-experience-of-a-next-genera|429-impact-of-procedural-experience-of-a-next-genera]]、[[来源/848-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-amulet-360-s|848-live-pre-recorded-case-presentation-amulet-360-s]]、[[来源/859-watchman-elite-and-amulet-360-device-design-inno|859-watchman-elite-and-amulet-360-device-design-inno]]

## 描述
Amulet 360是Abbott开发的下一代双封堵左心耳封堵器，在原有Amulet基础上进行了三项结构改进：lobe顺应性增强、双排更短锚定设计、远端螺钉内翻收纳。目前仍属研究性器械，正在VERITAS研究中接受评估，早期结果显示完全封堵率高于前代。
