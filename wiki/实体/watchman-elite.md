# WATCHMAN Elite

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/123-navigating-difficult-anatomy-shallow-appendages|123-navigating-difficult-anatomy-shallow-appendages]]、[[来源/411-next-generation-watchman-laac-system-watchman-el|411-next-generation-watchman-laac-system-watchman-el]]、[[来源/859-watchman-elite-and-amulet-360-device-design-inno|859-watchman-elite-and-amulet-360-device-design-inno]]

## 描述
WATCHMAN Elite 是 Boston Scientific 开发的下一代左心耳封堵器概念产品，在 WATCHMAN FLX 基础上采用单件式顺应性框架设计，优化了支架架构与器械-壁贴合稳定性，目标是扩大密封范围、减少边缘空隙并改善慢性完全封堵效果。该产品目前仍处于开发阶段，尚未上市销售。
