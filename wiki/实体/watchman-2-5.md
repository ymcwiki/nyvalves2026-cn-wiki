# Watchman 2.5

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/113-strategy-to-close-an-laa-that-is-adjacent-to-the|113-strategy-to-close-an-laa-that-is-adjacent-to-the]]、[[来源/611-left-atrial-appendage-occlusion-in-patients-with|611-left-atrial-appendage-occlusion-in-patients-with]]、[[来源/787-laac-tavr-with-case-examples|787-laac-tavr-with-case-examples]]

## 描述
Watchman 2.5 是 Boston Scientific 生产的第一代左心耳封堵器（LAAO），采用单排10个锚点（每个锚点间距2.5mm）、锐角钩爪及远端爪足开放设计。该装置被用于 WATCH-TAVR 试验的 LAAO 治疗组，后续已由升级版 WATCHMAN FLX 接替。
