# Medtronic Freestyle

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/022-transcathter-aortic-valve-in-valve-replacement-f|022-transcathter-aortic-valve-in-valve-replacement-f]]、[[来源/283-trilogy-transcatheter-aortic-valve-implantation|283-trilogy-transcatheter-aortic-valve-implantation]]、[[来源/313-lifetime-management-of-aortic-disease-assisted-b|313-lifetime-management-of-aortic-disease-assisted-b]]、[[来源/780-bilateral-leaflet-modification-with-ivl-ba-basil|780-bilateral-leaflet-modification-with-ivl-ba-basil]]

## 描述
Medtronic Freestyle 是 Medtronic 生产的猪异种移植无支架生物主动脉根部人工瓣膜，设计用于主动脉瓣及根部外科置换。其长期耐久性数据显示10年免于结构性退化率约95%，退化失败模式以主动脉反流为主。该瓣膜因无支架设计形成特殊解剖结构，植入后若发生晚期重度退化，可作为瓣中瓣（ViV）TAVI/TAVR 的操作平台，但同时也带来介入解剖上的额外挑战。
