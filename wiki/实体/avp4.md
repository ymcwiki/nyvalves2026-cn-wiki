# AVP4

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/055-percutaneous-closure-of-a-hemodynamically-signif|055-percutaneous-closure-of-a-hemodynamically-signif]]、[[来源/199-the-use-of-multi-modality-imaging-for-percutaneo|199-the-use-of-multi-modality-imaging-for-percutaneo]]

## 描述
AVP4（Amplatzer Vascular Plug 4/IV）是一款低轮廓血管封堵器，可经0.038英寸导丝兼容的诊断导管或Navicross导管递送。临床上用于封堵多种异常血流通道，包括冠状动脉至肺动脉瘘及瓣周漏（PVL），常用型号如8 mm。
