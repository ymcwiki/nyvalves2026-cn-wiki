# Epic 生物瓣

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/493-case-3-acute-aortic-bioprosthetic-valve-failure|493-case-3-acute-aortic-bioprosthetic-valve-failure]]、[[来源/845-transcatheter-mitral-valve-in-mis-aligned-surgic|845-transcatheter-mitral-valve-in-mis-aligned-surgic]]、[[来源/025-transcatheter-mitral-valve-in-valve-in-valve-imp|025-transcatheter-mitral-valve-in-valve-in-valve-imp]]

## 描述
Epic 生物瓣是 St. Jude Medical（现 Abbott）生产的外科生物瓣，有 23 mm 和 31 mm 等多种型号。两例患者均因植入后出现结构退化、重度反流，最终需行经导管瓣中瓣（ViV TAVR）治疗，一例为急诊手术。
