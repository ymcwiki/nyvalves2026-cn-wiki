# AVP III

## 基本信息
- Type: product
- Source: [[来源/236-a-primer-on-aortic-pvl-closure-assessment-planni|236-a-primer-on-aortic-pvl-closure-assessment-planni]]、[[来源/437-case-2-pvl-closure-strategies-how-to-avoid-hemol|437-case-2-pvl-closure-strategies-how-to-avoid-hemol]]、[[来源/012-management-of-severe-paravalvular-leak-during-sa|012-management-of-severe-paravalvular-leak-during-sa]]

## 描述
AVP III（Amplatzer Vascular Plug III）是一种多层镍钛合金血管塞封堵器，形态为非对称长圆形，适用于新月形或不规则形态的瓣周漏（PVL）缺损。其设计覆盖主动脉瓣及二尖瓣位置的 PVL 封堵，临床案例中已用于机械主动脉瓣旁缺损的介入封堵治疗。
