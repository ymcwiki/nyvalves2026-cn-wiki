# ATLAS Pivotal Trial

## 基本信息
- Type: project
- Source: [[来源/210-treatment-of-patients-with-narrow-left-ventricul|210-treatment-of-patients-with-narrow-left-ventricul]]、[[来源/781-altavalve-tmvr-in-mac-procedural-and-clinical-re|781-altavalve-tmvr-in-mac-procedural-and-clinical-re]]、[[来源/708-altavalve|708-altavalve]]、[[来源/382-navigating-the-challenging-neo-lvot-altavalve|382-navigating-the-challenging-neo-lvot-altavalve]]

## 描述
ATLAS 是评估 AltaValve 系统治疗二尖瓣反流（MR）的前瞻性多中心单臂关键性临床试验，目前正在入组，设主队列 350 例、MAC 队列 100 例。主要终点为 12 个月全因死亡与心衰住院的复合终点，同时评估器械长期安全性、有效性及 LVOT 梗阻情况。
